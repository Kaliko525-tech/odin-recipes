Lasagna

Description

This recipe comes to me from 3! not 1, not 2... But 3 Generations of handed down recipes. This is not an easy one to make but I am going to simplify it because this is not my job (read the README! its in the name, READ ME. so read it.).

A lasagna is one of those things, that given the time, can fill you up for days, make you feel like you have died and gone to heaven, even if you fuck it up. As mentioned above, I will be simplifying this recipe because it would take me far too long to type it out, it sadly is all in my head else I would have just copied and pasted it. This is an original recipe! so my ingredients list will look a little funny but maybe at a later stage I will come back and do a proper take.

Ingredients:

  • about 500g of Bolognese, if you don't know what bolognese is, then you have no right to be making lasagna untill you have made it! Also, the bolognese should be minimal saucy, we do need saucce but it musn't be too suacy, else you just end up with sloppy lasagna.
  • 500-750ml cheese sauce, this can be store bough, made from a packet, or homemade! which is best for this kind for thing and honestly its really easy to make, honestly takes 15 mins to make and that is including prep time. 5 mins to grate cheese and weigh ingredients, 10 mins to cook it and have a finished product.
  • 1 to 1 1/2 box of lasana sheets, that really is just highly dependent on what dish you make it in. we want to aim for something with a bit of heigh, at least enough for 4 layers , minimal 3.
  • some grated parmesan, grana padona, or what ever hard italian cheese you desire. Just not that crappy pre made powder parmesan, seriously! must be fresh hard cheese of the parmesan variety. No subsitutions!
  • Some grated cheddar cheese, probaly more than parmesan, but in both cases we are aiming for just enough to cover the top of the lasagna.

Directions:

  1. To start, lets get ourselve a nice deep rectangle oven proof dish, then lets pre-heat our over to 180C / 160c FAN / gas mark? (i feel like you use a gas oven then you will know what to use)
  2. Next, we are going start laying down our ingredients in layers starting with a thin layer of cheese sauce on the bottom of the dish, this is to prevent sticking , then a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by bolognese. Then we repeat, cheese sauce, lasagna sheets, bolognese. once you are on your fourth layer of lasana sheet, you can put a thin layer of bolognese, then some cheese sauce and finally the grated cheese.
  3. Lastly, is to put the dish on top of a flat tray, then put it into the oven for around 35-45 minutes, at this point you want to start checking if the pasta sheets are cooked, if they are, perfect take it out. if not leave it and check every 10 mins.
  4. Finally, let it cool to a reasonable temp before serving, top with basil if you like and ENJOY!
